What to Expect

Consultation

On the first day of installing new Solar Tek generators our survey team will consult with you and take measurements for optimal placement.

Upon choosing a finance option the installation begins right away. Our patented frame design allows us quickly secure new panels to almost any surface.

Inspection

Finishing the installation process our team contacts the county and dispatches a board certified inspector to finalize the process.
